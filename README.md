# NBA-Shot-Analysis

This was a project I completed as a avid NBA fan during the 2024 season with different areas of analysis using Statistics concepts in R

First I computed the average using differnet statistics estimates then confidence intervals to see what most of the players in the NBA shoot like barring outliers

Then we ran through differrent estimates performing sensitiviy analysis on a sample population looking at bias, variance and the mean sample error while looking at bootstrap as an alternative computation method 

A big part of sports in the regular season is that teams play the same amount of games at home vs away and sually the home team is favoured, so I dug into the data to see if there truly was a thing as "home advantage" and then conducting a hypotehsiss test with p values to incidcate our conclusion holds

Lastly, during commercial breaks during games, the sports coverage shows a player's shot charts in that particualr game of where they made and missed their shots, so I wanted to see it for the whole NBA. 
We created a court visualization, and a heatmap to see where are the players shoooting better and worse which can be used to draw up plays to get more shots in higher efficiency zones. As well as looking at the free throw line, 3 pointer from the top of the key and center court to see what the particular averages were at those positions which can be done for any position on the court
